AMERICAN PILSNER

WarFlag's American Pilsner was created to bring back the amazing American flavor lost over the last century. We poured hundreds of recipe variations to bring you the most flavorful American style pilsner in the country. Our Pilsner begins with sweet honey characters accented with spicy and grassy hop notes. About Us

War Flag Brewing, established in 2014, is a family owned and operated beer company based in Brooklyn, New York. The art of brewing and the importance of patriotism and civil liberties were key components and the driving forces behind the creation of the/our brand. War Flag’s iconic logo is a symbol to patriots everywhere who stand against tyranny and love liberty. As New Yorkers we are proud to offer you a taste of liberty as we raise our glass with you. Long live the republic, cheers !
